About Me

  

I am Jeila B. Ubando and I have decided to dedicate a website to my favorite KPOP band, Tomorrow X Together. I have been a fan of them since September 26, 2020. In my lowest times, they are the only people I have that's why I'm very thankful to them. Their music was able to have a big impact in my life. They are the reasons I wanted to keep on going in life.

   

I am always updated on their posts in Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, Vlive, Weverse, and more. I also have collected all of their albums, posters, lightstick, and more.

     

My room is also filled with colorful posters of TXT. The posters lighten up my mood. The colors of the posters also fits my room well.

HER JOURNEY WITH TXT

The five boys have already became a big part of her life, that's why whenever she achieves something, she always dedicates it to them. Every time she's sad, she listens to their music to make her happy. Every time she's discouraged, she makes them her inspiration.
